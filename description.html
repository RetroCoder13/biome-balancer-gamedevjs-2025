Biome Balancer

Coded in pure JavaScript by RetroCoder
Graphic Design by Zaperno
Concept by Ella

In Biome Balancer, your job is to save the displaced animals throughout the world which have been taken from their biomes by robots
The robots are wandering around the map, moving the animals towards the mainframe
If you find the keycard in the map, you can disable the mainframe which deactivates the hindering robots

Everytime you play the game the map is randomly generated along with animal spawns which makes every play different

WASD controls
M to open the map
C to open the animal compendium
Escape to exit the map and compendium
E to pick up an animal
R to drop an animal
