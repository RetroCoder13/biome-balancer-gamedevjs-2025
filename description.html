Biome Balance

Coded in pure JavaScript by RetroCoder
Music by RetroCoder
Art by Zaperno
Idea by Ella

In Biome Balance, your job is to save the displaced animals throughout the world which have been taken from their biomes by robots

WASD controls
M to open the map
C to open the animal compendium
Escape to exit the map and compendium
E to pick up an animal
R to drop an animal
